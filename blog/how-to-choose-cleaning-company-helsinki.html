How to Choose a Cleaning Company in Helsinki — Expert Tips

Nordic Globe·26.3.2026·5 min read

Helsinki has hundreds of cleaning companies, but not all are created equal. Here's how to separate the professionals from the rest.

5 Must-Have Criteria

  1. Tilaajavastuu Registration: This is non-negotiable in Finland. It proves the company pays taxes and follows labor laws.
  2. Platinum AAA Rating: Only the most financially stable companies earn this. Nordic Globe has held it since 2021.
  3. ISO Certifications: ISO 9001 for quality management, ISO 14001 for environmental responsibility.
  4. Proven Track Record: Look for companies with 10+ years of experience and verifiable customer reviews.
  5. Comprehensive Insurance: Full liability coverage protects your business.

Red Flags to Avoid

Watch out for companies with no public reviews, no website, cash-only payment, and no business ID (Y-tunnus). These often indicate unregistered operators.
